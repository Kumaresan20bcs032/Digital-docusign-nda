6. Fees

6.1 Fee based on Percentage of Consideration

(a) Subject to sub-clause (b) below, the Vendor must pay to MXC (and/or its nominees) the equivalent of:

(i) in respect of a Buyer or a Buyer’s Associate, six percent (6%) of the total aggregate value of the Consideration which the Vendor (and/or the Vendor Associates) has agreed to receive from a Buyer and a Buyer Connection(or a Buyer Associate);

in respect of a Transaction (referred to as “Fees”).

(b) Subject to the structure of the Transaction and the Consideration, the Fees may be satisfied in different forms subject to MXC’s written agreement. Unless otherwise agreed by MXC in writing, in the event that the Consideration under the Transaction is or includes:

(i) (Cash) Cash paid to the Vendor (and/or a Vendor Associate), the Vendor must satisfy the payment of the Fees by way of cash payment to MXC’s nominated bank account in clause 6.3;

(ii) (Securities) Securities issued to the Vendor (and/or a Vendor Associate), the Vendor must satisfy the payment of the Fees by way of either:

(A) if the Securities are not subject to mandatory restrictions from a regulated securities exchange, procuring the immediate transfer to MXC (and/or its nominees) of Securities in the same proportion and structure which the Vendor receives the Securities; or

(B) if the Securities are subject to mandatory restrictions from a regulated securities exchange, then once the relevant restriction period has expired, procuring the immediate transfer to MXC (and/or its nominees) of Securities in the same proportion and structure which the Vendor receives the Securities.

[Example: If the Vendor enters into a Transaction, and the Consideration was $100,000 cash, five million fully paid ordinary shares, one million class A performance rights and one million class B performance rights. Then (subject to the Vendor and MXC not mutually agreeing otherwise), the Vendor must pay and/or procure the payment/issue of the following to MXC (and/or its nominees) in satisfaction of the Fees at completion of the Transaction:

  • • $6,000 cash (being 6% of the total cash consideration);
  • • 300,000 fully paid ordinary shares (being 6% of the total number of ordinary shares being issued as Consideration);
  • • 60,000 class A performance rights and 60,000 class B performance rights (being 6% of the total number of each class of performance rights issued).]

(iii) (Earn-in or Joint Venture) if the Transaction is an earn-in and/or joint venture arrangement and the includes the Buyer(s) (or any third parties) paying Consideration to the Vendor (and/or a Vendor Associate) in respect of the Transaction, then the Vendor must satisfy the Fees by way of paying or transferring to MXC in respect of Consideration, cash or Securities (based on either 6% for an Introduced Buyer, and a Buyer Connection, of the cash or total number of Securities being issued as Consideration) in the same manner as set out in sub-clauses (i), (ii) above or (iv) below;

(iv) (Deferred Consideration): to be paid in cash and/or Securities after the initial completion of the Transaction, the Vendor must satisfy the payment of the Fees by way of procuring the payment or transfer to MXC (and/or its nominees) of its portion of the cash or Securities (in the same proportion which the Vendor receives) when the Vendor receives the deferred consideration.

Notwithstanding the requirements of this clause, the Vendor and MXC may (in their absolute discretion) mutually agree a cash payment to be paid by the Vendor to MXC (and/or its nominees) in substitution of any or all of the Fees that may be settled by way of cash, Securities, deferred consideration or future JV Expenditure.

(c) Except in respect of restricted Securities Fees related to JV Expenditure (the timing for payment of which is provided for in sub-clause (b)(ii)(B) above), the Vendor must pay the Fees to MXC (and/or its nominees), or must procure the payment of the Fees to MXC (and/or its nominees) from the Buyer (or Buyer Associate or relevant third party), on the same day on which the Vendor (and/or the Vendor’s Associates) receive any part of the Consideration in respect of a Transaction (Payment Date).

(d) For the avoidance of doubt, the total Fees due and payable by the Vendor to MXC under this clause is based on the total value of the Consideration which the Vendor (and/or Vendor Associates) may receive under the Transaction, even though some or all of the Consideration may be conditional or contingent on future actions (of third parties) at some time after Completion. However, the Vendor’s obligation to pay the Fees to MXC is subject to the Payment Date, being the date on which the Vendor (and/or the Vendor’s Associate) actually receives that part of the Consideration.
